Rect &  fixed 
)
inlinestatic

helper to shift and mitigate any intersection of two rectangles

note that any conditional shifting, e.g., to check for violating the outline, to apply only shifting in the direction of the lowest shift required etc may (and will) result in circular moves between different bbs; for example, when shifting considers the lowest shift and a new bb has to be shifted between two abutting bbs, the new bb will be shifted back and forth; thus, greedy shifting has to follow a strict shifting direction, i.e., upwards
